Content multiplication · change the format, not the message

✕ Bad strategy

You post once and never touch the topic again.

"I made a reel about X. It performed well. I never spoke about it again."
✓ Good strategy

That reel worked — now multiply it into 5 formats.

→ Carousel
→ Split screen (before/after)
→ Ranking countdown
→ Rating 1–10
→ Repost the original in 45 days

One idea. Five pieces. Done with AI in under 5 min per format.

Reel formats
Split screen — before vs. after (technique, lifestyle, results)
Rating 1–10 — score your shots, gear, or training days
Ranking countdown — top 3 mistakes, best shots, moments
Step-by-step tutorial — talking to camera, one concept
Carousel formats
Problem → solution → transformation (3-act)
"X things nobody tells you about..." (high saves)
Comparison — what most players do vs. what actually works
Myths vs. reality — each slide busts one assumption
Recycling rule: When a post outperforms your average, flag it. Within 2 weeks pick 2 formats above and remake it. Schedule the original repost 45 days out. One strong idea should produce at least 3 pieces — most creators leave this completely on the table.
